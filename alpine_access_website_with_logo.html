Safety & Compliance

Safety Is Not a Checkbox —
It's Our Culture

Every Alpine Access operation is governed by the IRATA International Code of Practice — the global benchmark for rope access safety. Our systems are documented, our technicians are certified, and our approach to safety is built into every job from the first risk assessment to the final debrief.

IRATA International Certified

All technicians hold current IRATA certification. Level 3 supervision is standard on every project — not an optional upgrade.

Site-Specific SWMS

Safe Work Method Statements prepared specifically for every job and every site. No generic templates. No cut-and-paste safety management.

Emergency Response Planning

Site-specific rescue plans are in place before any work at height commences. Emergency response is planned, rehearsed and documented.

Public Liability Insurance

Fully insured for commercial and industrial rope access operations Australia-wide. Certificates of currency available on request prior to mobilisation.

Workers Compensation

All personnel are fully covered under current workers compensation. All documentation available on request prior to site mobilisation.

Continuous Improvement Reviews

Post-job debrief and safety review on every project. Lessons are documented, shared and applied directly to future operations.
